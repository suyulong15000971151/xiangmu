Harmel Home Textile Design

Harmel Home Contract provides window, bedding, and upholstery solutions to the global hospitality industry with an extensive decorative range including performance textiles, manufactured finished products, and custom development capabilities.

Over the past three decades, Harmel Home Contract has furnished quality textiles for hotels the world, with hotel projects ranging from luxury five-star properties to value-oriented standards programs. Whether your project is for a one of a kind boutique hotel or a brand standard, Harmel Home Contract has the design staff, infrastructure and network of partners to support project demands.

Specializing in Custom Development

Creating custom fabrics is one of our specialties at Harmel Home Contract. We collaborate with our customers to develop project-specific designs and qualities that provide solutions within a specified budget and timeframe.
