MANUFACTURED PRODUCT

P/Kaufmann Contract has been a supplier to the hospitality industry since 1982, and designers’ one stop shop for customized fabricated product since 2004. We offer both domestic and offshore fabrication based on variables of quantity, price point and speed to market for your project.

Starting with printed or woven fabrics as a base, we develop and produce manufactured product. These items can be tailored to your specific project requirements. Our expanding range of products includes decorative throws and pillows in addition to embroidery, applique and quilting for top of the bed.

Please contact your P/Kaufmann Contract rep for quality examples and specification suggestions.

  • Bed skirt, Bed runner

    Coverlets utilize our wide array of available bedding fabrics including textured and dimensional fabrics such as our Quilted, Sonic Quilted, Matelasse and Chenille options. For duvet covers, begin with a lofty insert and pair with one of our sophisticated sheeting fabric options. Coverlets and Duvets are customizable to your specifications.
